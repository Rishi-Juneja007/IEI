About IEI Student Chapter

The Institution of Engineers (India) is the largest multi-disciplinary professional body of engineers, established in 1920 with its Headquarters in Kolkata and incorporated under Royal Charter in 1935.

The MITS Gwalior Student Chapter aims to promote the general advancement of engineering and engineering science and their application in India. We organize various workshops, seminars, and technical events to foster innovation and practical learning among students.
